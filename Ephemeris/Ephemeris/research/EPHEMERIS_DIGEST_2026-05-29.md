## Research Gaps Noted

- **JPL Horizons + audio mapping**: No dedicated papers or tools combine JPL Horizons ephemeris data with direct audio/OSC output. The closest related work is `AudiblePlanets` (GitHub), `HORIZONS.jl` / `lhorizon` (Horizons API clients), and the Auditory Virtual Observatory paper (arXiv:2405.11382, FITS2OSC pipeline). This appears to be an underexplored niche.
- **OSC + astronomy**: No papers explicitly use OSC in an astronomical research context beyond the Auditory Virtual Observatory (arXiv:2405.11382); OSC functions as infrastructure in broader sonification pipelines.
- **RTL-SDR + sonification combined**: No papers or repositories combine RTL-SDR radio data capture directly with real-time sonification output — the two domains remain separate.
- **Celestial mechanics audio synthesis**: The synthesizer `AudiblePlanets` is the only project mapping live orbital geometry to sound synthesis parameters in real time.
